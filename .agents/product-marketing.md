# Product Marketing Context

Last updated: 2026-06-11

## Product Overview

**One-liner:** La so tinh hoa helps Vietnamese readers create a free tu vi chart, learn how to read it, and optionally unlock deeper paid interpretations.

**What it does:** The app creates tu vi charts from birth information, explains chart concepts through evergreen articles, offers date-fortune support, and routes engaged readers toward saved charts and paid/coin readings. The public experience should feel calm, trustworthy, and useful rather than sensational.

**Product category:** Vietnamese tu vi chart tool, astrology/fortune education, personal reflection tool.

**Product type:** Consumer web app with free chart creation, content hub, logged-in saved charts, coins, and paid readings.

**Business model:** Free educational funnel into account creation, saved chart usage, coin topups, and paid readings.

## Target Audience

**Primary audience:** Vietnamese adults 30-60 who are curious about tu vi, want to understand a personal chart, or are preparing for family, work, relationship, or timing decisions.

**Primary use case:** Create a chart, understand the main structure, then decide whether a deeper reading is worth unlocking.

**Jobs to be done:**

- "Help me create a correct tu vi chart without feeling overwhelmed."
- "Help me understand what this chart section means in plain Vietnamese."
- "Help me check whether a date or decision needs more caution."
- "Help me decide if a paid interpretation is useful for my situation."

## Problems & Pain Points

**Core problem:** Tu vi information online is often scattered, exaggerated, hard to verify, or written in specialist language.

**Why alternatives fall short:**

- Some sites overwhelm users with tables and jargon before explaining what matters.
- Some content promises certainty about fate, money, marriage, or career.
- Some tools create a chart but do not guide readers toward the next useful step.

**Emotional tension:** Readers want insight and reassurance, but they do not want to feel manipulated or confused.

## Differentiation

**Key differentiators:**

- Free chart creation is connected to educational content and practical CTAs.
- Articles explain cause-and-context, not only isolated star meanings.
- The app keeps payment/coin gates separate from public education and avoids guaranteed claims.
- Automation prioritizes quality and retention over mass content output.

**Why customers choose us:** They can start free, learn in simple language, and only go deeper when the context feels relevant.

## Objections

| Objection | Response |
| --- | --- |
| "Tu vi websites are too hard to read." | Use plain Vietnamese, clear sections, and guided next steps. |
| "Will this be accurate for me?" | Explain that birth data, chart context, and modifiers matter; invite the user to check their own chart. |
| "Is this just generic content?" | Add structured tables, context notes, and links to the chart flow instead of generic advice. |
| "Will I be forced to pay?" | Let users create and explore a free chart before choosing paid readings. |

**Anti-persona:** Users looking for guaranteed predictions, medical/financial certainty, or mass-generated yearly fate claims.

## Customer Language

**Words to use:** la so tu vi, lap la so, xem ngay tot xau, giai la so, doc la so, gio sinh, cung Menh, van han, nen can nhac, boi canh, doi chieu.

**Words to avoid:** chac chan, bao dam, doi doi, phan quyet, so menh co dinh, lam giau nhanh, cam ket ket qua.

## Brand Voice

**Tone:** Calm, respectful, practical.

**Style:** Plain Vietnamese for adults, short explanations, no hype, no fear-based copy.

**Personality:** Trustworthy, thoughtful, grounded, helpful.

## Goals

**Primary business goal:** Grow qualified traffic that leads readers into chart creation and later paid readings.

**Key conversion action:** Create a free chart at `/#lap-la-so`.

**Secondary actions:** Read related articles, save a chart, return for date fortune, unlock a deeper reading.

**Measurement focus:** Organic impressions/clicks, article engagement, internal clicks to chart creation, account creation, saved charts, paid reading unlocks.
